Hi, my name is Carol.

I used to study Life Sci but stumbled upon coding in my second year and was absolutely nuts about it.

That summer, I took a course at Bitmaker, a coding bootcamp for web development. Realizing how much I loved programming, I transfered to Mathematics at the University of Waterloo.

I am a fullstack web developer with an interest in UI/UX design by day and an oyster connoisseur by night.
