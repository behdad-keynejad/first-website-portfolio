Hello. So here's my life story:

I started out in Biomedical Science with plans to pursue Optometry. I never really considered programming in high school, so I decided to try it out for fun by taking a functional programming course, attending my first hackathon and learning basic HTML and CSS from Team Treehouse. I thought I was passionate about Science but I was even more excited about programming! I decided to make the career switch and spent my free time learning as much as I could. I read blogs daily and followed up with tech news. Currently, I am enrolled in Bitmaker's Web Development Immersive course covering all of web and loving every single second of it! These past months have been one hell of a journey, but I'm glad I took the jump.

Grocery Runner

JUNE 30 2016

GroceryRunner is a grocery delivery platform to help free up time from dreaded chores. Users can browse through thousands of items and add them to their cart. Then, they simply checkout and select the date and time they would like their delivery. Past grocery lists are saved and used to track your spendings, savings and breakdown of purchases.

Tools: Paper & Pen (Wireframes), Sketch (High Fi Mockups)

Seat Yourself

JUNE 23 - JUNE 27 2016

There were so many bugs. Bugs everywhere. The bugs were good though, they helped me develop better debugging skills.
