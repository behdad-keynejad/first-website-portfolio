GroceryBuddy

GroceryBuddy is a mobile application that helps students save money on groceries. Students can create a list of items and checkout their basket to find the cheapest grocery store that carries their products within a selected radius. This mobile app was designed for a pitch competition held by CGI at the Canadian Undergraduate Technology Conference.
